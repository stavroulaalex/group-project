Monachus Monachus Seal
          About Mass: 320 kg (Male, Adult), 300 kg (Female, Adult) Class:
          Mammalia Family: Phocidae Species: M. monachus Food: Small fish,
          lobsters and octopus Habitat: Lives mainly in Greek seas, although
          there are other important populations in the Mediterranean Life span:
          They live up to 30 years, and females give birth to one pup each year.
          Over the last century their numbers have declined dramatically because
          there mostly killed by fishermen, who thought that they ate fish from
          nets and also from the pollution or overfishing.
